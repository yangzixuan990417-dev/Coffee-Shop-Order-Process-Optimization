# Power BI Dashboard Notes

The Power BI dashboard supports the To-Be process by providing visibility into order volume, sales performance, product demand, and potential inventory pressure.

## Dashboard Components

- Total Orders: shows overall transaction volume.
- Total Sales: shows revenue performance.
- Sales by Product Category: identifies high-performing product categories.
- Orders by Date: shows daily order trends.
- High-Demand Products for Stock Monitoring: identifies products that may require closer inventory monitoring.
- Store Location Filter: allows users to compare performance across different store locations.

## Connection to Process Improvement

In the redesigned To-Be process, inventory is automatically deducted after each transaction and low-stock alerts are generated when stock falls below the reorder level. Although the current dataset does not include real inventory levels, the dashboard uses product demand as a proxy to identify items that may require closer stock monitoring.
